Absolute Permission Transparency

BEE requests strict access limitations. We protect your billing detail and private messages from exposure.

What BEE Can Access

  • ✓
    Read WhatsApp Account Settings

    Verify WhatsApp status, profile display name details, and verification states.

  • ✓
    Transmit Pre-Approved Templates

    Send template transactional updates and notifications to contacts who chose to receive them.

  • ✓
    Monitor Message Sync Logs

    Audit log message states (Delivered, Read) of outbound templates.

What BEE Cannot Access

  • ✕
    Access Facebook Passwords

    BEE never sees or handles your Facebook administrative account passwords.

  • ✕
    Read Personal Text Conversations

    Private chat logs and databases are strictly encrypted. We cannot view them.

    Access Meta Billing Cards

    We have zero visibility into payment profiles, ad accounts, or billing cards on Facebook.
